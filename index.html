About

Dr. B. Muthusenthil is the Professor and Head of the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at SRM Valliammai Engineering College, Chennai. With over 23 years of academic and research experience, he specializes in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, IoT, and Network Security. A Post-Doctoral Researcher from Wookyoung Information Technology, South Korea, and a Ph.D. graduate from Anna University, he has led several funded projects under AICTE, DST, and TNS&T. His research focuses on developing AI-driven, secure, and intelligent systems for healthcare, automation, and societal applications.
